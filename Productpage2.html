First registration:1982

When MGB production was sadly coming to an end. To commemorate this, the company made a limited edition run of only 1000 cars. 580 Hard Top Coupe’s and only 420 of the more desirable Roadster. All finished in a unique colour for each model. This stunning Roadster was registered in 1982 and is in incredible time warp condition finished in a beautiful and unique Bronze metallic with those tell tale ‘LE’ Stripes.

The original owner took the wise precaution of having the car fully ‘rustproofed’ in order to seal and protect the bodywork. Sadly most MG’s were not as fortunate and so succumbed to rust, the result for our car is that it now is in ‘virtually new condition ‘

This car is simply breath-taking, having only covered a genuine 24000 miles from new, it looks and drives almost like the day it was produced. This MGB has been perfectly stored and an ongoing regime of protection and care brings the car to today over 40 years later.

This limited edition MGB Roadster LE comes with the original service pack, and history file. It is fitted with an Unleaded Head, Electronic Distributer and has just been treated to new tyres all round. In preparation for the next lucky owner who buys this incredibly rare car, we will provide a fresh service and MOT and of course our warranty.
